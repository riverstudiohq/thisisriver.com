A little
About me!

Welcome! My name is River Ramirez, and it is a pleasure to be able to share my work with you! Through my undergraduate education I strive to explore the intersection between technology, design, and marketing and further explore my interest in how music can be experienced beyond listening through interaction and emotion. My work spans product design, visual direction, and concept development, and is often centered around artists, audiences, and digital culture. I currently work to build projects that reimagine how people create and connect with music and research the impacts of user-experiences in sound and digital media.

Overall, I have an immense passion for learning and exploring new ways I can expand my knowledge and skills in the interdisiplinary fields of science, technology, music, design, and more.

Skills
I Offer

UI/UX Design

I design intuitive, interfaces and experiences that prioritize clarity, flow, and accessibility; creating designs that feel both functional and intentional. As I propel my career, I hope to refine my ability to integrate relevant contemporary ideals into my designed products to engage the intersection of new-generation audiences while still appealing to traditional user-experiences.

Web Development

I develop responsive, performance-driven websites using modern front-end technologies. My approach blends structure and thoughtful interaction, ensuring that my designs translate smoothly into code as demonstrated in this personal portfoilio. I continue to develop these skills not only in an acadmeic setting, but through freelance work building websites for music artists and small businesses.

Creative Direction

I develop/brainstorm visual and conceptual direction for digital experiences, and artist development where I work to bridge the gap between design, sound, and experience, as presented in my project "ECHO". My process involves shaping mood and identity systems that guide a project's overall feel. I aim to create works that are emotionally resonant and visually consistent across mediums.

Music Production/Sound Design

I produce soundscapes, compositions, and personal works that span the enhancement of visual and interactive work, as well as self experession via music production, singing, song-writting, and more. My focus is on atmosphere, texture, and emotional tone—using sound as a narrative layer in digital and artistic projects.

Business and Marketing

I aim to integrate design and music production into business and marketing to create cohesive, multi-sensory brand experiences. My work focuses on aligning visual systems, sound, and strategy to strengthen brand identity and audience engagement across digital platforms.
